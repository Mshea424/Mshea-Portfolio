Im a software engineer, Atlanta resident, and recent graduate of General Assembly. Prior to studying software development, I worked for over 8 years in the construction industry as both a craftsman and a project manager. During this time, I realized that the world is becoming more and more tech-oriented, with computers offering efficient solutions to an increasing number of problems and tasks. When the opportunity presented itself, I left my construction role, and dedicated myself full-time to entering a career in tech. My goals as a software engineer are to become educated so that I can be on board with thought leaders, to participate in creating and providing these aforementioned tech solutions, and eventually to provide an easier way for others to learn and use tech.
